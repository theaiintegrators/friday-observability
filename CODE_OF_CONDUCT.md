# Code of Conduct
_The AI Integrators Community_

## 1. Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the Friday ecosystem a **harassment-free**, **inclusive**, and **respectful** experience for everyone.

We are committed to:
- Creating a positive environment  
- Encouraging learning and contribution  
- Supporting new contributors  
- Respecting diverse viewpoints and backgrounds  

---

## 2. Our Standards

### Positive behaviors:
- Showing empathy and respect  
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback  
- Being open to alternative ideas  
- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Helping others learn the system  

### Unacceptable behaviors:
- Harassment or discrimination  
- Personal attacks or insults  
- Publishing private information  
- Disruptive or toxic conduct  
- Spamming or trolling  

---

## 3. Responsibilities of Maintainers
Maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior  
- Taking fair action when violations occur  
- Removing contributions that violate this code  
- Temporarily or permanently banning offenders when necessary  

---

## 4. Scope
This Code of Conduct applies:
- Within project repositories  
- In GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions  
- In community spaces created by the project  
- In interactions involving official project communication  

---

## 5. Enforcement
Violations may be reported by contacting:

```
theaiintegrators@gmail.com
```

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.

Maintainers who fail to enforce the code of conduct may face consequences appropriate to their role.
